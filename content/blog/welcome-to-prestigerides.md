Welcome to the official PrestigeRides blog. We created this space to help buyers and sellers navigate the used car market in Sri Lanka with confidence.

## What You'll Find Here

We'll be publishing content across three main themes:

### For Buyers

- Step-by-step guides to inspecting used cars
- Common mistakes to avoid when purchasing
- Model-specific reliability reviews
- Financing and transfer advice

### For Sellers

- How to price your car correctly
- Photography tips to make your listing stand out
- What paperwork you need ready before selling
- How to handle negotiations

### Market Insights

- Price trends for popular segments
- Seasonal buying and selling patterns
- Import vs. locally purchased vehicles

## How to Use PrestigeRides

**Browsing listings** is free and requires no account. Use the filters on the [Browse page](/cars) to narrow down by make, model, year, and price.

**Listing your car** takes about five minutes. You'll need to create a free seller account, then fill in your car's details and upload photos. Every listing is reviewed by our team before going live to ensure quality.

**Contacting sellers** — each listing shows the seller's contact details once you're on the listing page. We recommend meeting in a safe, public location for any test drive.
